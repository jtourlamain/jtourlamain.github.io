Xamarin after Hello World: architecting and structuring your code!

By Jan Tourlamain • December 08, 2016 • 0 Comments

Last week I gave a Xamarin session to students of the PXL in Hasselt (Belgium). You can find a lot of tutorials about Xamarin, but most of the time they only point towards 1 specific topic. When writing a real world app you can’t get your boss satisfied with a hello world.

Meet Tobit. I’ve created a demo application with a full tutorial to grab your GitHub repositories. So how can you organise your repository, architect your Xamarin solution and make an app with resilient networking, authentication, caching,… it’s all there! All code, the tutorial and slides can be found on my GitHub on https://github.com/jtourlamain/Tobania.Xam.Tobit.

A big thanks to Tobania who sponsored the setup of this tutorial and the session itself.
